Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about QCanary pricing and plans.

What's the difference between Free and Solo?
Solo gives you 5 queues (vs. 1), 14-day history (vs. 24 hours), Slack alerts, and 25,000 events/day (vs. 5,000). It's for solo founders who need production monitoring without the team features.
Can I try before I buy?
Yes. The Free tier is free forever for personal projects. Use it to evaluate QCanary. When you're ready for production, upgrade to Solo or Team.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
We'll notify you at 80% and 100% of your limit. You can upgrade instantly from your dashboard. We don't hard-cut you off — we give you a grace period to decide.
Do I need a credit card for the free tier?
No. No credit card required. No time limit. The free tier is free forever for personal projects and evaluation.
Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?
Yes. You can switch at any time from your dashboard. Annual plans save 20%.
What's the difference between Solo and Team?
Team adds multiple projects, team member access (5 people), unlimited alert rules, webhooks, and API access. Solo is for one person. Team is for a group.
What's the difference between Team and Business?
Business adds SSO (SAML/OIDC), role-based access control, PagerDuty/OpsGenie integrations, priority support with a Slack channel, and usage analytics. If you have a dedicated DevOps or SRE team, Business is the right fit.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. 14-day money-back guarantee on your first paid invoice. No questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice (ACH, wire transfer) with annual contracts.
Is there a discount for startups or non-profits?
Yes. Startups under 2 years old and non-profits get 50% off Solo, Team, or Business for the first year. Contact us with proof of status.
